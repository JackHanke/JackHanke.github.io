I'm starting to understand why Lee Sedol no longer plays Go. 

As you might have been able to tell from this site, I like doing personal programming and math projects. I take a lot of pride in these little posts. Or maybe "took" is a better word.

The last year has been... draining. I've watched my passion become a $20 subscription. It feels awful. It's like a specter hanging over me saying "That project you did last month? That's a prompt now. Oh you studied hard to solve this problem? Did you run it by Claude?" I feel like a car just driven off the lot.

I'll continue to do these projects like I have before, regardless of LLMs. But I can't help but notice they no longer have the same luster.
